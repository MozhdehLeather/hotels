Government-Supported Dormitories

These budget-friendly options are different from normal hotels — they are government or municipality-supported, run by Kudumbashree & Guruvayur Devaswom. Perfect for short stays or when you just need a safe place to rest near the temple.

Advantages
  • Very affordable (₹200-₹400 per 12 hours)
  • Centrally located near temple entrances
  • Clean and secure facilities
  • Separate sections for men & women
  • No advance booking required (walk-in)
Limitations
  • Shared dormitory-style accommodation
  • Basic amenities only (no luxury facilities)
  • Charged per 12-hour blocks (not per night)
  • No online booking - first-come, first-served
  • May be crowded during peak seasons
